Manipur - The Jewel of India

Geography

Manipur is a northeastern state of India, bordered by Nagaland, Mizoram, Assam, and Myanmar. It is known for its **lush green valleys, beautiful lakes, and scenic landscapes**.

Culture

Manipur has a unique cultural identity, known for its **Manipuri dance, rich traditions, and festivals like Yaoshang and Ningol Chakouba**. The state is also famous for **Polo, which originated here**.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Imphal
  • Largest City: Imphal
  • Official Language: Meitei (Manipuri)
  • Famous Festivals: Yaoshang, Lai Haraoba, Ningol Chakouba
  • Major Rivers: Barak, Imphal
  • Chief Minister: N. Biren Singh

Tourist Attractions

Popular places to visit include **Loktak Lake (floating lake), Kangla Fort, Keibul Lamjao National Park (home to the rare Sangai deer), and Shirui Hills**.
