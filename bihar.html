Bihar - The Land of Enlightenment

Geography

Bihar is located in the eastern part of India, bordered by Uttar Pradesh, Nepal, West Bengal, and Jharkhand. It is known for its fertile land and historical significance.

Culture

Bihar is famous for its rich cultural heritage, folk traditions, and festivals like Chhath Puja. Hindi and Bhojpuri are widely spoken languages.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Patna
  • Official Language: Hindi
  • Famous Temples: Mahabodhi Temple, Vishnupad Temple
  • Popular Festivals: Chhath Puja, Sonepur Mela
  • Major Rivers: Ganges, Kosi
  • Chief Minister: Nitish Kumar

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Bodh Gaya, Nalanda University, Rajgir, Vaishali, and Madhubani.
