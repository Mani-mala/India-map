Himachal Pradesh - The Land of Snow

Geography

Himachal Pradesh is located in the northern part of India, bordered by Jammu & Kashmir, Punjab, Haryana, Uttarakhand, and Tibet. Known for its Himalayan ranges, it has numerous hill stations and valleys.

Culture

Himachal Pradesh is known for its vibrant culture, folk dances, music, and festivals. The state celebrates major festivals like Dussehra and Lohri. Hindi is the official language, but Pahari dialects are widely spoken.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Shimla
  • Largest City: Shimla
  • Official Language: Hindi
  • Popular Tourist Spots: Manali, Dharamshala, Kullu
  • Famous Temples: Jwalamukhi Temple, Baijnath Temple
  • Major Rivers: Beas, Sutlej
  • Chief Minister: Sukhvinder Singh Sukhu

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Manali, Kullu, Dharamshala, McLeod Ganj, and Rohtang Pass. These places are popular for trekking, skiing, and paragliding.
