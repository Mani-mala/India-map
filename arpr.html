Arunachal Pradesh - The Land of the Rising Sun

Geography

Arunachal Pradesh is the northeasternmost state of India, bordered by China, Bhutan, Myanmar, and the Indian states of Assam and Nagaland. The state is known for its **dense forests, mountain ranges, and diverse tribal cultures**.

Culture

Arunachal Pradesh is home to over **26 major tribes and numerous sub-tribes**. The state is famous for **Tawang Monastery, Losar Festival, and traditional tribal dances**.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Itanagar
  • Largest City: Itanagar
  • Official Language: English
  • Famous Festivals: Losar, Solung, Nyokum
  • Major Rivers: Brahmaputra, Subansiri
  • Chief Minister: Pema Khandu

Tourist Attractions

Popular places to visit include **Tawang Monastery, Sela Pass, Ziro Valley, Namdapha National Park, and Bum La Pass**.
