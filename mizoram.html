Mizoram - The Land of the Highlanders

Geography

Mizoram is a northeastern state of India, bordered by Myanmar, Bangladesh, Assam, Manipur, and Tripura. It is known for its rolling hills, dense forests, and breathtaking landscapes.

Culture

Mizoram has a rich tribal culture, influenced by its Mizo communities. The state is famous for its **Cheraw (Bamboo Dance)** and vibrant festivals like **Chapchar Kut and Mim Kut**.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Aizawl
  • Largest City: Aizawl
  • Official Language: Mizo (Lushai), English
  • Famous Festivals: Chapchar Kut, Mim Kut, Pawl Kut
  • Major Rivers: Tlawng, Chhimtuipui
  • Chief Minister: Lalduhoma

Tourist Attractions

Popular places to visit include **Phawngpui National Park (Blue Mountain), Vantawng Falls, Reiek, and Tam Dil Lake**.
