Chhattisgarh - The Land of Waterfalls

Geography

Chhattisgarh is located in central India, bordered by Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Telangana, Odisha, Jharkhand, and Uttar Pradesh. It is known for its dense forests and waterfalls.

Culture

Chhattisgarh is famous for its tribal heritage, folk dances like Panthi and Raut Nacha, and traditional festivals. Chhattisgarhi and Hindi are the widely spoken languages.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Raipur
  • Largest City: Raipur
  • Official Language: Chhattisgarhi, Hindi
  • Famous Temples: Bhoramdeo Temple, Danteshwari Temple
  • Popular Festivals: Bastar Dussehra, Madai Festival
  • Major Rivers: Mahanadi, Indravati
  • Chief Minister: Vishnu Deo Sai

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Chitrakote Waterfalls, Bhoramdeo Temple, Kanger Valley National Park, and Barnawapara Wildlife Sanctuary.
