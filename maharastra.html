Maharashtra - The Land of Marathas

Geography

Maharashtra is located in the western part of India, bordered by Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Telangana, Karnataka, and Goa. It has a long coastline along the Arabian Sea.

Culture

Maharashtra is known for its diverse culture, Lavani dance, Ganesh Chaturthi festival, and historical forts. Marathi is the official language.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Mumbai
  • Official Language: Marathi
  • Famous Temples: Siddhivinayak Temple, Trimbakeshwar Temple
  • Popular Festivals: Ganesh Chaturthi, Gudi Padwa
  • Major Rivers: Godavari, Krishna
  • Chief Minister: Eknath Shinde

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Gateway of India, Ajanta & Ellora Caves, Shirdi, Mahabaleshwar, and Lonavala.
