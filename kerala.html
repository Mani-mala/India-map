Kerala - God's Own Country

Geography

Kerala is located in the southwestern part of India, bordered by Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. It has a long coastline along the Arabian Sea and is known for its backwaters and lush greenery.

Culture

Kerala is known for its classical dance forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, as well as its unique festivals, such as Onam and Vishu. Malayalam is the official language.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Thiruvananthapuram
  • Official Language: Malayalam
  • Famous Temples: Sabarimala Temple, Padmanabhaswamy Temple
  • Popular Festivals: Onam, Vishu
  • Major Rivers: Periyar, Bharathapuzha
  • Chief Minister: Pinarayi Vijayan

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Munnar, Alleppey, Wayanad, Kovalam Beach, and Thekkady.
