Karnataka - The Land of Heritage

Geography

Karnataka is located in the southwestern part of India, bordered by Maharashtra, Goa, Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and Andhra Pradesh. It has a long coastline along the Arabian Sea.

Culture

Karnataka is known for its diverse culture, classical music (Carnatic music), and dance forms like Yakshagana. Kannada is the official language.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Bengaluru
  • Official Language: Kannada
  • Famous Temples: Virupaksha Temple, Murudeshwar Temple
  • Popular Festivals: Dasara, Ugadi
  • Major Rivers: Kaveri, Tungabhadra
  • Chief Minister: Siddaramaiah

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Hampi, Mysore Palace, Coorg, Jog Falls, and Gokarna.
