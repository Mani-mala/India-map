Madhya Pradesh - The Heart of India

Geography

Madhya Pradesh is located in central India, bordered by Uttar Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Maharashtra, Gujarat, and Rajasthan. It is known for its rich biodiversity and national parks.

Culture

Madhya Pradesh is famous for its classical music, tribal art, and architectural heritage. Hindi is the official language.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Bhopal
  • Largest City: Indore
  • Official Language: Hindi
  • Famous Temples: Khajuraho Temples, Mahakaleshwar Temple
  • Popular Festivals: Lokrang Festival, Tansen Samaroh
  • Major Rivers: Narmada, Tapti
  • Chief Minister: Mohan Yadav

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Khajuraho, Kanha National Park, Sanchi Stupa, Pachmarhi, and Ujjain.
