Punjab - The Land of Five Rivers

Geography

Punjab is located in the northern part of India, bordered by Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu and Kashmir, Rajasthan, and Pakistan. It is known for its fertile land and rich agricultural output.

Culture

Punjab is famous for its Bhangra dance, Giddha, Punjabi cuisine, and festivals like Baisakhi and Lohri. Punjabi is the official language.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Chandigarh (shared with Haryana)
  • Largest City: Ludhiana
  • Official Language: Punjabi
  • Famous Temples: Golden Temple, Durgiana Temple
  • Popular Festivals: Baisakhi, Lohri
  • Major Rivers: Sutlej, Beas, Ravi
  • Chief Minister: Bhagwant Mann

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Amritsar, Wagah Border, Jallianwala Bagh, Anandpur Sahib, and Patiala.
