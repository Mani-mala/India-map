Odisha - The Land of Temples

Geography

Odisha is located in the eastern part of India, bordered by West Bengal, Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh, Andhra Pradesh, and the Bay of Bengal. It is known for its rich culture and beautiful coastline.

Culture

Odisha is famous for its Odissi dance, classical music, Rath Yatra festival, and ancient temples. Odia is the official language.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Bhubaneswar
  • Largest City: Bhubaneswar
  • Official Language: Odia
  • Famous Temples: Jagannath Temple, Lingaraj Temple
  • Popular Festivals: Rath Yatra, Durga Puja
  • Major Rivers: Mahanadi, Brahmani
  • Chief Minister: Naveen Patnaik

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Puri, Konark Sun Temple, Chilika Lake, Simlipal National Park, and Bhitarkanika Wildlife Sanctuary.
