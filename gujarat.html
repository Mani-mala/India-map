Gujarat - The Land of the Lions

Geography

Gujarat is located in the western part of India, bordered by Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, and the Arabian Sea. It is known for its vast coastline and Gir National Park.

Culture

Gujarat is famous for its Garba dance, vibrant festivals like Navratri, and rich historical heritage. Gujarati is the official language.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Gandhinagar
  • Largest City: Ahmedabad
  • Official Language: Gujarati
  • Famous Temples: Somnath Temple, Dwarkadhish Temple
  • Popular Festivals: Navratri, Uttarayan
  • Major Rivers: Narmada, Tapi
  • Chief Minister: Bhupendra Patel

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Gir National Park, Rann of Kutch, Somnath, Dwarka, and the Statue of Unity.
