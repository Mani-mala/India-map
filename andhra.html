Andhra Pradesh - The Rice Bowl of India

Geography

Andhra Pradesh is located in the southeastern part of India, bordered by Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Odisha, and the Bay of Bengal.

Culture

Andhra Pradesh is known for its Kuchipudi dance, vibrant festivals, and delicious cuisine. Telugu is the official language.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Amaravati (de facto), Visakhapatnam (proposed)
  • Official Language: Telugu
  • Famous Temples: Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, Kanaka Durga Temple
  • Popular Festivals: Ugadi, Pongal
  • Major Rivers: Godavari, Krishna
  • Chief Minister: Y. S. Jagan Mohan Reddy

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Araku Valley, Tirupati, Vizag Beaches, Srisailam, and Lepakshi.
