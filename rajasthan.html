Rajasthan - The Land of Kings

Geography

Rajasthan is located in the northwestern part of India, bordered by Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, and Gujarat. It is known for its Thar Desert and historical forts.

Culture

Rajasthan is famous for its folk music, dance forms like Ghoomar and Kalbeliya, and grand heritage palaces. Rajasthani and Hindi are widely spoken languages.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Jaipur
  • Largest City: Jaipur
  • Official Language: Hindi
  • Famous Forts: Amer Fort, Mehrangarh Fort
  • Popular Festivals: Pushkar Fair, Desert Festival
  • Major Rivers: Chambal, Banas
  • Chief Minister: Bhajan Lal Sharma

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Jaipur, Udaipur, Jaisalmer, Jodhpur, and Ranthambore National Park.
