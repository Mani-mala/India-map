Delhi - The Heart of India

Geography

Delhi is the capital of India, bordered by Haryana and Uttar Pradesh. It is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and vibrant markets.

Culture

Delhi is famous for its Mughal heritage, street food, and festivals like Diwali and Eid. Hindi and English are widely spoken languages.

Key Facts

  • Capital: New Delhi
  • Largest City: New Delhi
  • Official Language: Hindi, English
  • Famous Monuments: Red Fort, India Gate, Qutub Minar
  • Popular Festivals: Republic Day Parade, Diwali
  • Major Rivers: Yamuna
  • Chief Minister: Arvind Kejriwal

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Lotus Temple, Humayun's Tomb, Akshardham Temple, and Chandni Chowk.
