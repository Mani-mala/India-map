Jharkhand - The Land of Traibal

Geography

Jharkhand is located in the eastern part of India, bordered by Bihar, West Bengal, Odisha, Chhattisgarh, and Uttar Pradesh. It is known for its rich mineral resources and dense forests.

Culture

Jharkhand is known for its tribal heritage, folk dances like Chhau and Jhumar, and vibrant festivals. Hindi is the official language, with a significant presence of tribal languages.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Ranchi
  • Official Language: Hindi
  • Famous Temples: Baidyanath Temple, Jagannath Temple
  • Popular Festivals: Sarhul, Karam
  • Major Rivers: Damodar, Subarnarekha
  • Chief Minister: Hemant Soren

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Netarhat, Dassam Falls, Betla National Park, Hundru Falls, and Parasnath Hill.
