Goa - The Pearl of the Orient

Geography

Goa is located on the western coast of India, bordered by Maharashtra and Karnataka. It has a long coastline along the Arabian Sea.

Culture

Goa is known for its vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and beautiful beaches. Konkani is the official language.

Key Facts

  • Capital: Panaji
  • Official Language: Konkani
  • Famous Churches: Basilica of Bom Jesus, Se Cathedral
  • Popular Festivals: Carnival, Shigmo
  • Major Rivers: Mandovi, Zuari
  • Chief Minister: Pramod Sawant

Tourist Attractions

Some famous tourist places include Baga Beach, Dudhsagar Waterfalls, Fort Aguada, and Chapora Fort.
